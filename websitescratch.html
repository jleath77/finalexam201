Bryce Harper

Bryce Harper: ESPN's 13th Ranked Player and Bleacher Report's 9th Ranked Player

  1. Career
    • Las Vegas High School
    • College of Southern Nevada
    • Drafted by the Washington Nationals in 2010 as the first overall pick
    • Signed with the Philadelphia Phillies in the 2018-2019 offseason
  2. Accolades
    • Youngest position player to play in an All-Star game
    • National League Rookie of the Year in 2012
    • National League MVP in 2015 and 2021
    • 7x All-Star
  3. Fun Facts
    • Third largest contract in the MLB
    • 310 Career home runs
    • Proud owner of the Atlanta Braves
